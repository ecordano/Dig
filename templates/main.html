Dig It: A Guide to Urban Agriculture

Our Inspiration

At Dig It we have a passion for improving and facilitating urban gardening. Major Philadelphia grocery stores charge high prices for fresh and nutritious produce imported from outside of the region. Meanwhile there are many empty lots throughout city that are not being used. We have discovered that by using empty lots to grow produce, many households would be able to obtain nutritious food at cheaper prices. Through study of past efforts and examinination their reasons for failure, we have created a better system called Dig It.

Or Goal

Dig It aims to solve the problem of costly and inaccessible produce by providing an application that gives step by step instructions on how to farm a community garden in Philadelphjia, with the intention that local communities will be able to grow some of their own food.
